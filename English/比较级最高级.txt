1:通常在形容词和副词的后面加  er est
	hard	harder	the hardest
2 词尾是不发音的单音节e时
	nice	nicer	the nicest
3 词尾是辅音+y,去掉y+er|去掉y+est
	dry	drier	the driest
4 重读闭音节结尾,双写最后一个字母+er|est
	hot	hotter	hottest
5 多音节和双音节,在形容词和副词前加
	加强:more,the most
	减弱:less,the least
	interesting 
	more|less interesting
	the most|the least interesting
6 不规则的
	good->better->the best
	many->more->the most
